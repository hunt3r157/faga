Our Story

Where Community Meets Competition

FAGA Chicago was born from a shared love of golf and the bonds of friendship. What started as a group of Filipino and American golfers teeing it up together in Chicago has grown into a thriving community — open to players of every nationality and skill level.

At our core, we believe golf is best experienced together. Whether you're a seasoned club member or a weekend warrior picking up the game, FAGA Chicago is your home on the course. We're here for the competition, the camaraderie, and the post-round laughs.

The League

15 Weeks. 15 Courses. One Champion.

Weekly Rounds

Play a different course each week across the Chicagoland area. Our schedule features a curated rotation of the region's finest layouts — from championship tests to hidden gems.

Handicapped Scoring

Fair play is everything. As a registered CDGA member club, we use official handicap indexes through CDGA.org so every golfer competes on a level playing field.

Season Championship

Points accumulate all season long, building toward our season-ending championship. Compete for bragging rights, awards, and the title of FAGA Chicago League Champion.

All Are Welcome

FAGA Chicago embraces golfers of every background, age, and ability. Whether you carry a scratch handicap or you're just learning to break 100, there's a spot on the tee sheet for you.
